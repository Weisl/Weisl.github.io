Simple

Simple Tools make game art creation easier. They are straightforward, flexible, and designed to fit into different workflows. Whether you’re managing cameras, creating physics colliders, exporting assets, or batch renaming, Simple Tools help you get the job done.

Who is Simple?

Simple is my passion project, born from my deep interest in pipelines and problem-solving. With years of experience in the AAA game industry, I’ve encountered many challenges in game art creation. Simple Tools are my solutions to those challenges — designed to make workflows smoother and tackle the issues I face in my regular work as a 3D Environment Artist.

Continuous improvement is a priority at Simple. Your feedback helps me refine and enhance these tools to better meet your needs. Check out the tools in the stores below and see how they can make your game art creation process more efficient.
