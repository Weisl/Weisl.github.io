Streamlined Export for Collections from Blender!

What is Simple Export?

Simple Export makes exporting assets from Blender quick and easy. It helps you manage export settings, create custom paths, and ensures that your assets are exported correctly to engines like UE, Unity, Godot, and more. With Simple Export, you can streamline your workflow and keep everything organized.

Feature Overview

  • Easy Export Path Management: Quickly set and adjust export paths for your collections.
  • Support for Multiple Engines: Export assets directly to UE, Unity, Godot, and other engines.
  • Customizable Export Presets: Apply consistent export settings across all collections.
  • Multi-Collection Exporting: Export all collections, selected collections, or individual collections easily.
  • Integrated UI: Access and adjust export settings directly from the 3D Viewport.
  • Flexible Naming Options: Customize the naming of exported assets to fit your pipeline.
  • Directory Management: Automatically create export directories if they don't exist.
  • Batch Exports: Export multiple collections or entire scenes in one go.

Why Simple Export?

Simple Export removes the hassle of managing export settings manually. It gives you an all-in-one solution to export assets quickly and consistently, directly within Blender. Focus on your creative work, and let Simple Export handle the exporting process.

  • Efficient: Automate tasks and save time.
  • Consistent: Ensure assets are always exported correctly.
  • Flexible: Customize paths, filenames, and presets.
  • Convenient: Manage everything within Blender.
